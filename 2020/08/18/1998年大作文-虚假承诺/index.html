1998年大作文 虚假承诺

发表于 2020-08-18 更新于 2020-08-20 分类于 Learning
本文字数： 2.3k 阅读时长 ≈ 2 分钟

范文原文

  The past several years have been witnessed a phenomenon in which a variety of promises have been arising from people in all walks of life. Regretfully, quite a lot of promises are sheer nonsense, as the hen in the given cartoon commits herself to laying eggs which are round without any angles and corners, contain anything normal egg contains.
  Odd and funny as they sound, such false promises can be seen and heard everywhere in our country. Some administration departments promise to perform their tasks effectively and fairly without taking any bribes; some manufacturing units guarantee to turn out product of good qualify; some commercial enterprises swear to provide genuine commodities and polite and enthusiastic services. Can you find anything new and substantial other than their assumed obligations in these “promises”? I presume your answer will be negative. As a matter of fact, their intension in making such commitments are nothing but to put on a civilized disguise to please and deceive the public.
  I dare say that corruption and cheating are causing damage to our society both materially and morally. But the hen and her like should know that dishonest behaviors help no one to survive the intense competition of a market economy system. They should remember the old saying, “Honesty is the best policy”.

大纲

  1. (总结图画含义再描述)第一段对图画进行描述，注意并不一定要一字一句完全直译，结合社会现象，在归纳论点的基础上描述图画：a variety of promise, quite a lot of promises are sheer nonsense, as…..
  2. 第二段展开论述列举原因，本文中列举为什么要虚假承诺的原因可能有些难，所以可以采用举例子列举现实生活的实例的方式只强调一个原因：Some…, some…., some…, please and deceive the public.
  3. 第三段提出建议，对于描述社会现象的题目：
    1. 可以点出对社会的不利影响：causing damage to our society
    2. 并不需要提很多建议，可以先描述如果不按建议作会怎么样：help no one to survive…
    3. 最后以牢记诚信为结尾，虽然不是实际性的如何行动的建议。

重难点词汇和句式

词汇

注意区分quiet和quite：
quiet：安静，轻声，不显眼的；quite：比较，相当，表强度
bribe：指贿赂，行贿
manufacturing units：生产厂家
administration departments：管理部门
commercial enterprises：商业企业
genuine commodities：正品，genuine表示真的，名副其实的，可信赖的
obligation：表示义务责任，与responsible不同，该词偏向于本应该做到的事情
civilized disguise：文明的伪装，disguise表示掩饰，假扮，伪装
deceive：欺骗，使人误信，误导他人
honesty：n. 诚信；honest：adj. 诚信的；dishonest：adj.不诚信的
intense：adj. 强烈的，表示程度加深；intention：n. 表示目的

句式

引出社会现象
The past several years have been witnessed a phenomenon which/in which…..
承诺的多种表达
promise to do…; guarantee to do…; swear to do…; commit oneself to doing sth..
履行职责可以采用任务而不是责任+不接受贿赂：
perform their tasks effectively and fairly without taking any bribes.
目的在于，引出某个现象的原因：
intention in doing sth.. is/are nothing but to….
披着伪装做某事…
put on a adj. disguise to do….
描述某个社会现象给社会带来了不利影响
I dare say that n. and n. are causing damage to society both materially and morally.
