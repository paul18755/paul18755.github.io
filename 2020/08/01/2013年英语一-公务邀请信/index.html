2013年英语一 公务邀请信

发表于 2020-08-01 分类于 Learning
本文字数： 1.3k 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟

范文原文

Dear Professor Smith,
  On behalf of the “Talent” speech contest committee, I’m writing to invite you to be our new judge. As the core of “Foreign Culture Festival“, this school-wide contest is to be held in Students’ Auditorium on May 12(nest Sunday). And we all believe that you can give our competitors some enlightening comments as a both native-English speaker and erudite professor. We will cover the expenses for your food, accommodation and transportation fares. Specially, the event will start around 7:00 p.m. and last for 2 hours at most. At the end of it, we will have some hand-made presents for each judge and competitor.
  Once again, we sincerely hope you can be the judge for our competition. Please RSVP before this weekend.

Your sincerely
Li Ming
Chairman of "Talent" speech contest committee

提纲

公务邀请信，开头称呼应正式-Professor

  1. 对于“我”:
    文段开头自我介绍+表明写作目的：On behalf of the…..，I’m writing to…..
  2. 对于“事件” :
    介绍具体细节：
    • 什么比赛：As the core of…. 表示重要性，
    • 在哪里：Students’ Auditorium
    • 什么时候：May 12(nest Sunday)，具体7:00 p.m.and last for 2 hours at most.
    • 比赛可以额外说的：hand-made presents
    • 负责费用
  3. 对于“对象”:
    表明邀请原因：
    • 做什么：enlightening comments
    • 为什么：native-English speaker and erudite professor
  4. 结束语：
    • 再强调一遍写作目的
    • 一般邀请信需要及时知道对方的意见，可以附上回复日期

学习的词组和句式

词组

school-wide：全校性的，全国性的为 national而不是nation-wide
As the core of… ：表示重要性，作为什么的核心
Students’ Auditorium：学生大礼堂
Foreign Culture Festival：外国文化节
RSVP：表示期望尽快回复

句式

表示承担食宿交通费用
We will cover the expenses for your food, accommodation and transportation fares.
表示比赛举办
contest is to be held in….
