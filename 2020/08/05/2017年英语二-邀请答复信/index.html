2017年英语二 邀请答复信

发表于 2020-08-05 分类于 Learning
本文字数： 1.3k 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟

范文原文

Dear Professor Williams,
  I am extremely honored to accept your invitation to give a presentation to a host of foreign students on Chinese culture.
  Here are the key points of my presentation. First of all, compared with Westerners, Chinese people have traditionally paid more attention to harmonious relationship between human beings and nature. “Nature and Man are one” is a familiar statement in traditional Chinese ideology. In addition, in Chinese thought, much emphasis is laid upon moral cultivation, refinement of temper/disposition, and the spiritual enlightenment in the part of individual rather than upon logical reasoning.
  My appreciation to you for your generous help is beyond words. I am looking forward to hearing from you soon.

Your sincerely
Li Ming

提纲

  1. 第一段感谢邀请+邀请目的：extremely honored to accept….
  2. 第二段就中国文化以及中西方的差异展开两点来说
    • 大：整体文化差异 traditional Chinese ideology——重视和谐harmonious relationship
    • 小：个体文化差异 Chinese thought——重视修身养性心灵感悟spiritual enlightenment
  3. 第三段可以再次表示感谢+期待回信（学习感谢的句式）

学习的单词和句式

单词

host：n. 会议 v. 支持，主办
Westerners：西方人
harmonious：和谐的，和
Nature and Man are one：天人合一
traditional Chinese ideology：中国传统思想
refinement of temper/disposition：修身养性
spiritual：心灵上的，宗教上的

句式

表承上启下转折：
first of all…., in addition…..
会议上作关于…的演讲：
give a presentation to a host of sb. on sth.（on后面接主要内容）
更重视…. 用名词形式表示重视：
much emphasis is laid upon ….. rather than ….. ：表示更多的重视（名词）被放在了….上面
表示感谢：
my appreciation to you for ….. is beyond words.：表示感谢无以言表，加深程度。
