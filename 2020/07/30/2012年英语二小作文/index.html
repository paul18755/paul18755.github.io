2012年英语二小作文

发表于 2020-07-30 更新于 2020-08-01 分类于 Learning
本文字数： 1.1k 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟

范文原文

Dear Sir or Madam,
  I brought an electronic dictionary from your online store on January 1st. But after using it for a short period time, I found it has some problems.

  In the begin with, the battery needs recharging after a mere 2 hours of use. This means that I need to carry my charger with me when I go out for a day. In addition, the buttons on the electronic dictionary are so small that is impossible to type anything with them. Personally, I find this to be completely unacceptable.

  Therefore, I would like either to change for another model that works better or a full refund. Your help will greatly appreciate.

Your sincerely,
Zhang Wei

提纲

  本文是一篇针对网络购物商家的投诉信，属于公文写作应当用正式的语言。

  1. 第一段：自我介绍和目的，开门见山说自己是顾客且发现产品存在问题。首句说明情况围绕”买东西”来展开三点：
    • 是什么：electronic dictionary
    • 在哪里：from your online store
    • 什么时候：on January 1st
  2. 第二段提出产品问题贴合题目给出的提纲“complaint”，两个方面：硬件电池+按键设计
  3. 第三段表明自己的诉求贴合提纲“demand a prompt solution”，也是提出两点：换货+退款

学习单词与词组

单词与词组：

using sth. for a short period time: 表示用某物仅仅用了很短的时间
battery needs recharging：电池需要充电
a mere 2 hours of use：名词形式表示使用两个小时
change for a full refund：全额退款

句式：

衔接上下文
To begin with, ….., In addition, ….：首先……，此外……，
表示感谢的句式
Your help is greatly appreciate：适用于提出要求后表示的客套话，感谢帮助
表示对…完全无法接受
Personally, I find this to be completely unacceptable：适用于投诉信表示对….完全无法接受
